Experimental Terminology

Term Definition
P1 Postnatal day 1 (neonatal mouse hearts)
P2 Postnatal day 2 (neonatal mouse hearts)
P8 Postnatal day 8 (transitional stage in cardiac development)
Sham Control surgery without myocardial infarction (MI) induction
MI Myocardial infarction model (experimental heart injury)
PSD# Post-surgery Sequencing Date, ie how many days after the surgery were the hearts sequenced

Statistical Terminology

Case Meaning
P1 vs P8 Comparing expression profile between P1 individuals against P8 individuals (base case)
MI vs Sham Comparing expression profile between MI individuals against Sham individuals (base case)
